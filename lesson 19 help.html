How to change your Netflix password
Visit netflix.com/loginhelp.
Select Email.
Enter your email address and select Email Me.
Follow the steps in the password reset email you receive. The email will typically arrive within a few minutes, and contains a link that will sign you in to Netflix automatically.

how to update email in netflix

Change your email address or password
Sign in to Netflix.com.
Hover over the profile icon in the top right hand corner.
Select Account.
Select Change email or Change password.
Enter your new email/password
